How to use with Google
Saving to Google Docs After each entry, tap "Copy to clipboard" then open your Google Doc and paste (Cmd/Ctrl+V). The entry will be formatted and ready to go. Pin your Doc to your browser bookmarks for quick access.

Adding to Google Calendar Tap "Add priorities to Calendar" — it opens Google Calendar with tomorrow's priorities pre-filled as an all-day event called "Tomorrow's Priorities". Just hit Save.

Install on your phone In Safari: tap the Share button → "Add to Home Screen". In Chrome: tap the menu → "Add to Home Screen". The app will work offline once installed.
